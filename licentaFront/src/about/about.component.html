About this Project

This platform was developed as part of a Bachelor’s Thesis at the Faculty of Automation and Computer Science – Technical University of Cluj-Napoca .

The research explores how decentralized technologies and swarm intelligence—specifically Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)—can be used to improve energy consumption efficiency within smart grids.

By integrating blockchain-based smart contracts with an Angular-based frontend, this project enables real-time optimization, transparency, and user participation in energy management.

It aims to serve as a practical and academic contribution to the development of sustainable, decentralized energy systems.
